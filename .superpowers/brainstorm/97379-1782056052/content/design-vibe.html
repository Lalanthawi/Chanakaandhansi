What's the design vibe for Chanaka & Hansi's wedding site?

Sri Lankan weddings have beautiful range — pick the direction that feels right

🪷

Traditional Kandyan

Rich crimson & gold, lotus motifs, Batik patterns, elegant & ceremonial. Feels like a true Lankan celebration.

🌿

Tropical Garden

Lush greens, hibiscus & frangipani, warm ivory. Outdoor garden party meets tropical paradise.

✨

Modern Sri Lankan Fusion

Contemporary minimal design with subtle Lankan touches — terracotta, gold accents, clean typography. Elegant & fresh.

🌊

Coastal / Beach

Ocean blues, sandy tones, coconut palms. Sri Lanka's stunning coastline as the backdrop inspiration.
